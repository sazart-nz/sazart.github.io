Sarah – Mixed Media & Fluid Art

I work across gouache, watercolour, pen and ink, acrylic paint/inks, markers, and whatever mixed‑media surfaces catch my interest. Oils are still on the horizon — something I’ll explore when it feels right.

I have aphantasia, so I don’t see images in my mind. There are no internal pictures guiding me. Everything happens directly on the surface. I respond to what appears, make things up on the fly, or use reference materials when I need structure.

My art play is intuitive and exploratory. I follow colour, movement, and texture, letting each layer shift the direction of the piece. This portfolio brings together fluid abstracts, character pieces, and mixed‑media experiments created simply because I enjoy the process of making and playing with colour.
